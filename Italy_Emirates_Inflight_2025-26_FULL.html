Decorations

Crunchy Chickpeas | DNATA:001 CAT:M19064

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (cooked and drained): 600 g
  • Olive oil: 50 g
  • Ground cumin: 5 g
  • Paprika: 5 g
  • Salt: 5 g
  • Ground black pepper: 3 g

Method

  • Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  • Toss the chickpeas with olive oil, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  • Spread the chickpeas in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  • Roast for 25-30 minutes, shaking the pan halfway through until crispy and golden.

Spiced Walnuts | DNATA:002 CAT:M20980

Ingredients

  • Walnut halves 20 g
  • Honey 10 g
  • Smoked paprika 2 g
  • Cinnamon 1 g
  • Salt 2 g

Method

  • Preheat the oven to 160°C.
  • Toss the walnuts with honey, smoked paprika, cinnamon, and salt.
  • Spread on a lined baking tray and bake for 10 minutes, stirring halfway through.
  • Cool completely before serving.

Cumin-Mint Tapioca Chips | DNATA:003 CAT:M25110

Ingredients

  • tapioca pearls 200 g
  • water 1 L
  • ground cumin 5 g
  • dried mint 5 g
  • salt 5 g

Method

  • Boil tapioca pearls in 1 L water until fully translucent.
  • Drain and mix with ground cumin, dried mint, and salt.
  • Spread thinly on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  • Dehydrate at 60°C for 6-8 hours until completely crisp.
  • Break into irregular chips before plating.

Roasted Cashew Nuts with Harissa Spice | DNATA:004 CAT:M21604

Ingredients

  • Cashew nuts (whole, raw) 100 g
  • Harissa paste 30 g
  • Olive oil 15 ml
  • Salt 2 g

Method

  • Preheat oven to 160°C.
  • Toss cashew nuts with harissa paste, olive oil, and salt until evenly coated.
  • Spread nuts on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  • Roast for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally for even cooking.
  • Let cool before serving.

Fried Eggplant Julienne (Only Skin) | DNATA:005 CAT:M08719

Ingredients

  • Eggplant skin (cut into thin julienne) 100 g
  • Vegetable oil (for frying) 500 ml
  • Salt 5 g

Method

  • Heat the vegetable oil to 160°C.
  • Dry the eggplant skin with a paper towel to remove excess moisture.
  • Fry in small batches until crispy, about 1-2 minutes.
  • Drain on paper towels and season with salt while still hot.

Lamb Ragout Salounah | DNATA:103 CAT:M06979

Ingredients

  • Lamb shoulder or leg (boneless, trimmed, large dice): 1000 g
  • Onion (sliced): 200 g
  • Garlic (minced): 20 g
  • Fresh ginger (grated): 15 g
  • Tomato (chopped): 300 g
  • Tomato paste: 40 g
  • Potato (peeled, large cubes): 300 g
  • Carrot (peeled, thick slices): 200 g
  • Green chili (optional): 1 pc
  • Water or lamb stock: 1.2 L
  • Vegetable oil: 60 g
  • Salt: 14 g
  • Black pepper (ground): 3 g
  • Turmeric powder: 4 g
  • Ground cumin: 4 g
  • Ground coriander: 4 g
  • Ground cinnamon: 1 g
  • Dried lemon (loomi, whole, pierced): 1 pc
  • Fresh coriander (chopped): for garnish

Method

  • In a heavy pot, heat oil and brown the lamb in batches until well-colored. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same pot, sauté onions until golden. Add garlic and ginger, and cook 1 minute.
  • Stir in tomato paste and chopped tomatoes. Cook until thick and reduced.
  • Add the spices, loomi, and return lamb to the pot. Stir to coat well.
  • Pour in stock or water to cover. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat, cover, and simmer for 60–90 minutes until lamb is tender.
  • Add potatoes, carrots, and chili (if using). Simmer uncovered for another 30 minutes, or until vegetables are tender and sauce is slightly thickened.
  • Adjust seasoning and remove loomi. Finish with fresh coriander.

Rice Machbous | DNATA:104 CAT:M16883

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice (soaked 30 min, drained): 1000 g
  • Onion (sliced): 300 g
  • Garlic (minced): 20 g
  • Fresh ginger (grated): 20 g
  • Tomatoes (chopped): 300 g
  • Tomato paste: 50 g
  • Vegetable oil or ghee: 100 g
  • Whole black lime (loomi): 2 pcs (pierced with a knife)
  • Cinnamon stick: 1 pc
  • Green cardamom pods: 5 pcs
  • Cloves: 4 pcs
  • Bay leaf: 2 pcs
  • Cumin seeds: 5 g
  • Turmeric powder: 5 g
  • Ground coriander: 5 g
  • Garam masala: 5 g
  • Salt: 18 g
  • Black pepper (ground): 3 g
  • Water or stock: 1.8 liters
  • Fresh coriander (chopped, optional for garnish): 10 g

Method

  • In a large heavy pot, heat the oil or ghee, then sauté the onions over medium heat until deep golden brown.
  • Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for 1 minute until aromatic.
  • Stir in the tomatoes and tomato paste, cooking until the tomatoes break down and the paste darkens slightly.
  • Add all whole spices (black lime, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, bay leaf, cumin) and toast for 1–2 minutes.
  • Add the ground spices (turmeric, coriander, garam masala), salt, and black pepper. Cook for 30 seconds.
  • Pour in the water or stock, bring to a boil, and simmer for 10 minutes to infuse the broth.
  • Stir in the soaked, drained basmati rice, return to a boil, then reduce to low heat. Cover tightly and cook for 15–18 minutes, or until rice is tender and liquid absorbed.
  • Remove from heat and rest covered for 10 minutes. Fluff with a fork.

Vegan Barley Risotto with Mushrooms | DNATA:105 CAT:M19927

Ingredients

  • pearl barley 1.6 kg
  • olive oil 100 g
  • shallots, finely chopped 200 g
  • garlic, minced 50 g
  • vegetable stock 2.5 l
  • salt 10 g
  • black pepper 5 g

Method

  • Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  • Sauté shallots and garlic until soft and translucent.
  • Add barley, stirring for 2 minutes until lightly toasted.
  • Gradually add vegetable stock, stirring frequently, allowing the barley to absorb the liquid before adding more. Continue for 30-35 minutes until creamy and cooked through.
  • Season with salt and black pepper.

  • Celery, diced small 50 g
  • Courgette, diced small 50 g

  • Black pepper, ground 2 g

Method

  • Infuse the Stock:
  • Heat vegetable stock with curry powder, salt, and black pepper.
  • Prepare the Couscous:

  • Cover and let it absorb the liquid for 5 minutes.
  • Fluff & Finish:
  • Drizzle with olive oil and fluff with a fork.

Saffron Risotto | DNATA:107 CAT:M25639

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice 1.8 kg
  • Vegetable stock 4.5 L
  • White onion, finely chopped 200 g
  • Smoked tofu, crumbled 300 g
  • Olive oil 100 ml
  • Saffron threads 2 g
  • Salt 15 g
  • Black pepper 3 g

Method

  • Heat the vegetable stock and keep it warm.
  • In a large pan, heat olive oil and sauté the finely chopped onion until soft.
  • Add the Arborio rice and toast for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  • Gradually add the warm vegetable stock, stirring continuously until the rice is al dente.
  • Dissolve the saffron threads in a small amount of warm stock and incorporate them into the risotto.
  • Season with salt, black pepper and add the tofu.

Vegan Courgette Risotto | DNATA:108 CAT:M06675

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice 1.4 kg
  • Courgettes 1 kg (grated)
  • Onion 200 g (finely chopped)
  • Garlic 30 g (minced)
  • Vegetable broth 3 liters
  • Olive oil 100 ml
  • Lemon zest 10 g
  • Fresh parsley 50 g (chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper 5 g

Method

  • Sauté aromatics: In a large pan, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  • Toast the rice: Add Arborio rice and stir for 1-2 minutes until slightly translucent.
  • Add courgettes: Stir in the grated courgettes and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Cook the risotto: Gradually add vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously until absorbed before adding more. Repeat for 15-18 minutes.
  • Season and finish: Stir in lemon zest, salt, and black pepper. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Garnish: Sprinkle with fresh parsley before serving.

Basmati Rice with Dill | DNATA:109 CAT:M02963

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice 1.5 kg
  • Water 2.25 L
  • Salt 15 g
  • Fresh dill (chopped) 50 g
  • Butter or olive oil 50 g

Method

  • Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  • In a large pot, bring water to a boil and add salt.
  • Add the rice, reduce the heat to low, and cover. Cook for 15 minutes.
  • Turn off the heat and let it rest for 10 minutes, covered.
  • Fluff the rice with a fork and mix in the chopped dill and butter.

Asparagus Spelt Risotto | DNATA:110 CAT:M25596

Ingredients

  • Spelt grain 1.8 kg
  • Vegetable stock 3 L
  • Olive oil 100 ml
  • Shallots (finely chopped) 150 g
  • Asparagus (tips and stems, chopped) 500 g
  • Butter 150 g
  • Vegan hard cheese (grated) 150 g
  • Salt 15 g
  • Black pepper 5 g

Method

  • Heat the vegetable stock and keep it warm.
  • In a large pan, heat the olive oil and sauté shallots until translucent.
  • Add the spelt and toast for 2 minutes over medium heat.
  • Gradually add warm stock, stirring continuously, and cook for 35 minutes.
  • When almost done, add chopped asparagus and cook for another 5 minutes.
  • Stir in butter and vegan hard cheese, then season with salt and black pepper.

Jeera Rice | DNATA:111 CAT:M10414

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice (soaked 30 minutes, drained): 1000 g
  • Ghee or vegetable oil: 80 g
  • Cumin seeds (jeera): 12 g
  • Bay leaf: 1 pc
  • Green cardamom pods (lightly crushed): 2 pcs
  • Cloves: 2 pcs
  • Water: 1.6 L
  • Salt: 18 g

Method

  • Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain thoroughly.
  • In a wide saucepan or heavy pot, heat the ghee over medium heat. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cardamom, and cloves.
  • Sauté the spices for 30–60 seconds, until fragrant and the cumin begins to crackle.
  • Add the drained rice and sauté gently for 1–2 minutes, coating each grain in the spiced ghee.
  • Add water and salt, bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to low.
  • Simmer for 15–18 minutes, or until rice is tender and water is absorbed.
  • Remove from heat and rest covered for 5–10 minutes, then fluff gently with a fork.
