1. Apple & Sultana Dressing

Ingredients:

  • Apple juice – 200g
  • White balsamic – 20g
  • Mustard – 10g
  • Sultana – 30g
  • Salt – 3g
  • Black pepper – 1g

Method:

Combine all ingredients in a bowl and whisk until fully emulsified. Rest before serving.
