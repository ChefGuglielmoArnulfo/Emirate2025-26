Crunchy Chickpeas | DNATA:001 CAT:M19064

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (cooked and drained): 600 g
  • Olive oil: 50 g
  • Ground cumin: 5 g
  • Paprika: 5 g
  • Salt: 5 g
  • Ground black pepper: 3 g

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Toss the chickpeas with olive oil, cumin, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Spread the chickpeas in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  4. Roast for 25-30 minutes, shaking the pan halfway through until crispy and golden.
